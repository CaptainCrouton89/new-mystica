MYSTICA - PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: October 2025

1. OVERVIEW
Mystica is a location-based adventure game. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

2.1 Location Data
- When in Use: Mystica collects your precise location only while the app is actively running
- Purpose: To identify nearby battle locations, enemies, and adventures for gameplay
- Storage: Location data is NOT stored on our servers
- Sharing: Location data is NOT shared with third parties

2.2 Device Information
- Device type, model, OS version
- Unique device identifier (for anonymous usage tracking only)
- Game progress and inventory data

2.3 Optional Information
- If you contact support, we may collect your email and support messages
- Analytics: We collect anonymous gameplay metrics (items found, battles won, time played)

3. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION
- To provide core gameplay functionality
- To improve game features and performance
- To detect and prevent technical issues
- To respond to support requests

4. DATA RETENTION
- Location data: Deleted in real-time; never stored
- Device data: Retained while app is installed
- Game progress: Stored i

5. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES
This app uses the following third-party services:
- Google Maps SDK: For map display and location services
- Cloudflare R2: For image/asset storage (anonymously)
- Replicate/OpenAI: For AI image generation (your location is NOT sent to these services)

6. YOUR RIGHTS
- You can revoke location permissions at any time in Settings > Mystica > Location
- You can delete your local game data by uninstalling the app
- Location data is never stored, so there is no data to delete

7. CHILDREN
Mystica does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we learn we have done so, we will delete such information promptly.

8. SECURITY
We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.
